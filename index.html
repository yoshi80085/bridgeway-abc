✦ Our Courses

The right class for every age

Our curriculum is designed to grow with your child — starting from kindergarten and continuing right through junior high school.

📚 Our core programmes use the Hand in Hand series — a proven spiral curriculum that revisits and deepens learning at every stage.
Kindergarten

Little Hands (Levels 1–3)

Our gentlest introduction to English — songs, stories, and play-based activities that build a joyful first connection with the language.

🎈 Ages 3–5👥 Small groups
 Primary School

Hand in Hand (Starter – Level 6)

A step-by-step spiral curriculum that reviews and builds. Each level adds new vocabulary, grammar and communication skills on a solid foundation.

📚 Ages 6–12👥 Small groups
 Junior High

First Hand Access / My Next Grammar

Bridge to academic English — reading, writing, grammar, and conversation skills that prepare students for high school and beyond.

📖 Ages 12–15👥 Small groups
 Adults

Adult Conversation

Friendly, relaxed English conversation classes for parents and adults. Build practical confidence for travel, work, or everyday life.

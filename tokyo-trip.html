Bridgeway goes to Tokyo

For the first time, our students stepped off the airplane and into one of the world's most exciting cities — using English the whole way. From navigating to exploring Tokyo Global Gateway, every moment was a real-world English experience wrapped in the energy of Japan's capital.

✦ Highlight

An immersive English world inside Tokyo

Tokyo Global Gateway is a state-of-the-art English immersion facility where every zone, every staff member, and every interaction happens entirely in English. For our students, it was like stepping into another country — right in the heart of Tokyo. A perfect fit for Bridgeway kids ready to put their English to the real test.
